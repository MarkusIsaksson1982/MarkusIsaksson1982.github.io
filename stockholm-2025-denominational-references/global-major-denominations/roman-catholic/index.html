Notes

Key documents include the Catechism of the Catholic Church, Vatican II (especially Lumen Gentium and Unitatis Redintegratio), and papal encyclicals. In Sweden, the Catholic Church is represented by the Diocese of Stockholm. Globally, it maintains ecumenical dialogues with Orthodox, Anglican, Protestant, and Pentecostal communities.
