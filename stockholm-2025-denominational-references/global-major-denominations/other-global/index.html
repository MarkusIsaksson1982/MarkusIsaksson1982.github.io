Overview

This category encompasses Christian denominations and movements not included in the primary global families (Catholic, Orthodox, Protestant, Anglican, Pentecostal). Examples include Adventist, Anabaptist, Oriental Orthodox beyond major jurisdictions, independent evangelical networks, and emerging global movements. Membership varies widely, with local, regional, and international structures.
