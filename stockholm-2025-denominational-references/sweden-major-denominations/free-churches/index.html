Overview

Sweden's Free Churches encompass Evangelical, Baptist, Methodist, and Pentecostal denominations outside the Church of Sweden. They emphasize personal conversion, charismatic worship, Bible-centered teaching, and congregational autonomy. Though not formally major participants at Stockholm 2025, they represent the broader "Word of Faith"-theology spectrum for local research.

Theological Highlights

  • Word of Faith emphasis on divine blessing, healing, and prosperity theology
  • Strong focus on evangelism, small group discipleship, and lay leadership
  • Charismatic worship style with contemporary music and preaching
  • Congregational governance and autonomy, cooperative networks across Sweden

Notes

These churches provide insight into Sweden's non-established evangelical landscape. Including them in research allows for better understanding of local church diversity, even if they do not participate directly in Stockholm 2025 ecumenical events.
