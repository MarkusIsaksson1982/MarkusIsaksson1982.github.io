Swedish Pentecostal Movement (Pingst)

Theological Foundations

Classical Pentecostal theology with Swedish contextual expressions
  • Statement of Faith: https://www.pingst.se/tro
  • Fundamental Truths: https://www.pingst.se/grundlaggande-sanningar
  • Baptism in the Holy Spirit: https://www.pingst.se/andens-dop
  • Divine Healing: https://www.pingst.se/gudomlig-helande

Key Theological Distinctives: Four-fold Gospel (Jesus as Savior, Sanctifier, Healer, Coming King), baptism in Holy Spirit with initial evidence of speaking in tongues, divine healing, premillennial second coming. Strong emphasis on personal relationship with Jesus and Spirit-filled living.

Liturgical Practices

Contemporary worship with Pentecostal spiritual gifts emphasis
  • Worship Guidelines: https://www.pingst.se/gudstjanst
  • Spiritual Gifts in Worship: https://www.pingst.se/andegavor
  • Music and Praise: https://www.pingst.se/musik

Sacraments: Two ordinances (Water baptism by immersion, Holy Communion). Strong emphasis on baptism in Holy Spirit, divine healing, prophecy, and other spiritual gifts in congregational worship. Contemporary music and spontaneous Spirit-led worship elements.

Organizational Structure

Congregational autonomy with cooperative fellowship structure
  • National Organization: https://www.pingst.se/organisation
  • Local Churches: https://www.pingst.se/lokala-kyrkor
  • Leadership Structure: https://www.pingst.se/ledning

Governance Model: Cooperative fellowship of autonomous local churches. Democratic decision-making with elected leadership. Strong emphasis on local church autonomy while maintaining denominational unity through shared theology and mission cooperation.

Social Teaching

Holistic gospel emphasis - spiritual and social transformation
  • Social Action: https://www.pingst.se/social-handling
  • PMU (Swedish Pentecostal Mission): https://www.pmu.se/
  • Addiction Recovery: https://www.pingst.se/beroendevard
  • Youth and Family: https://www.pingst.se/familj-ungdom

Current Priorities: Holistic mission combining evangelism with social action, international development through PMU, addiction recovery ministries, youth engagement, integration support for immigrants. Growing environmental consciousness within Pentecostal theology.

Ecumenical Relations

Selective ecumenical engagement based on evangelical priorities
  • World Assemblies of God Fellowship: Global Pentecostal Fellowship
  • Swedish Evangelical Alliance: https://www.evangeliska.se/
  • Pentecostal European Fellowship: https://pef-europe.org/
  • Stockholm 2025 Participation: https://www.pingst.se/stockholm2025

Ecumenical Stance: Selective participation in evangelical ecumenical movements. Growing openness to broader Christian cooperation while maintaining distinctive Pentecostal identity. Active participant in Stockholm 2025 through Josefina Gniste's worship leadership.

Swedish Context

Immigrant church origins now fully Swedish-integrated movement
  • Historical Development: https://www.pingst.se/historia
  • Cultural Integration: https://www.pingst.se/svensk-kontext
  • Immigration Ministry: https://www.pingst.se/flyktingarbete

Contemporary Identity: From early 20th-century revival origins to established Swedish denomination. Strong growth among immigrant communities while maintaining Swedish cultural integration. Significant youth engagement through contemporary worship styles and social media presence.
