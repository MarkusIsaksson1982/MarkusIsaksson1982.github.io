### Student Form ("Elevformulär") Breakdown

This form ("Main_assignment_1.jpg") must be filled out and submitted. It requires the following information:

1.  **Name** and **Class**.
2.  **Topic:** "Jag tänker tala om:" (I intend to talk about:).
3.  **Source(s):** "Jag har använt följande källa/källor:" (I have used the following source(s):).
4.  **Outline:** A structured plan for the speech.
    *   **Introduction** ("Inledning")
    *   **Main Body** ("Huvuddel") - preferably in bullet points.
    *   **Conclusion** ("Avslutning")
5.  **Technical Aid:** "Jag tänker använda följande..." (I intend to use the following technical presentation aid:).
