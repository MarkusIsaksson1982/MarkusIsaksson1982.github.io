ARTI (AI Rhetorical Text Integration)

Role: Interdisciplinary hub across curricula

Integration Level: Very High

Implementation Pathways

  • Year 1: Teacher AI literacy, Svenska–ARTI pilots
  • Year 2: Expansion to Matematik and Programmering, AI audits
  • Year 3: Cross-curricular rollout, evaluation, refinement

Assessment Frameworks

  • AI Interaction Checklist (transparency, bias, citation, reflection)
  • Dual Path Rubrics (digital/analog parity)
  • AI Audit Framework (minority language & democracy impact)

Minority Language Protection

Languages: Sami, Meänkieli, Romani chib, Swedish Sign Language

Strategies: Detection, Preservation, Advocacy, Education
